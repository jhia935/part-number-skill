Acoustic Noise Suppression

The ferroelectric ceramic materials commonly used in high
capacitance-type MLCCs can result in mechanical distortions
when an electric field is applied. With an AC voltage, the ca-
pacitor vibrates and transmits this vibration to the substrate
creating an audible sound. In response to this issue, TAIYO
YUDEN has developed acoustic noise-suppression dielectric
materials that are used in commercialized capacitors to reduce
the acoustic noise.
