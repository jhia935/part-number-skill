                                              SimSurfing is a web application
                                              which allows circuit designers to
                                              see our components’ characteristics
                                              data, and to select the one that best
                                              suits the requirement.

                                                 View and download data
                                                 You can see various characteristics graphs for our
                                                 products with easy operation, or download data files
                                                 including s-parameter, spice models, etc.


                                                 Simulate circuit conditions
                                                 Simsurfing includes advanced equivalent circuit models
                                                 which show the characteristics data close to actual
                                                 measurement (for some components including MLCC
                                                 & RF inductors).


                                                 Compare characteristics
                                                 Easily compare characteristics data on the same graph.
