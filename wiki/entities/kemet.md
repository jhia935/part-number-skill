# KEMET

A US-headquartered passive-component manufacturer, acquired by [[yageo]] in 2020 and now operating as a Yageo subsidiary. Active in tantalum, aluminum, film, and ceramic capacitors. The MLCC line is a mid-tier player globally, but KEMET's public technical materials and application notes are widely cited as MLCC training references.

## MLCC technology leadership
- **ArcShield** ([[kemet-arcshield]]) — patented high-voltage MLCC architecture with internal shield electrodes for arcing suppression without external coatings. 500/630/1000 V_dc, 0603–2225, 1 nF–560 nF, X7R BME.
- **Flex termination (FT-CAP)** — Ag-epoxy layer between barrier and external electrode, raising flex bend capability to 5 mm.
- **Floating-electrode / open-mode** designs for fail-open behavior.

## Public reference value
The KEMET / PSMA training decks ([[kemet-mlcc-design-and-characteristics]], [[psma-ceramic-capacitor-basics]]) are the canonical open reference for MLCC construction, EIA classification tables, BME dielectric thickness progression, failure modes, and flex-crack mitigation hierarchy.
