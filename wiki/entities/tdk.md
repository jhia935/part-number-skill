# TDK Corporation

Japanese passive-component manufacturer, headquartered in Tokyo. A Tier-1 MLCC manufacturer alongside [[murata]] and [[samsung-electro-mechanics]]. Major presence in automotive and industrial MLCCs.

## Product portfolio (MLCC)
- **CGA / CGB / CKG** series — automotive grade (AEC-Q200).
- **C-series** (commercial general purpose) — case sizes 008004 through 5750 metric.
- **CeraLink** — anti-ferroelectric capacitors for high-current power electronics (PbLaZrTi-based, not BaTiO₃).
