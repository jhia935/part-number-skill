# Yageo

Taiwanese passive-component manufacturer (HQ Taipei). Significant MLCC presence both organically and via acquisitions — Yageo owns [[kemet]] (acquired 2020) and Pulse Electronics. Active participant in AEC-Q200 specification development (LeAnna Weakley, Alfred Chen on Rev E sub-committee).

## Product portfolio (MLCC)
- Yageo-branded commercial MLCC series.
- KEMET-branded specialty: [[kemet-arcshield]] HV, FT-CAP flex termination.
