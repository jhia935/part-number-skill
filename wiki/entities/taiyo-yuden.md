# Taiyo Yuden

Japanese passive-component manufacturer, headquartered in Tokyo. The fourth Tier-1 MLCC house alongside [[murata]], [[samsung-electro-mechanics]], and [[tdk]]. Historically known for industry-leading high-capacitance miniaturization.

## Notable milestones
- 2022/2023: doubled capacitance in the 0402 case size — characteristic Taiyo Yuden roadmap of pushing volumetric efficiency at small case sizes (per EE Times reporting).

## Product portfolio (MLCC)
- **Y-series / GMK / EMK / LMK** — broad commercial lineup.
- Industrial / automotive AEC-Q200 grade variants.
