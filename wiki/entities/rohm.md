# ROHM Co., Ltd.

Japanese semiconductor manufacturer (Kyoto). Not an MLCC producer in the Tier-1 sense, but ROHM's switching-regulator app notes are cited extensively for **MLCC application guidance** by power supply designers — including the DC-V characteristic comparisons referenced in [[rohm-ceramic-cap-app-note]].

## Relevance to MLCC research
ROHM apps engineers publish detailed treatment of MLCC pitfalls when designing in MLCC bypass / output caps for buck converters: feedback-loop stability, ripple current self-heating, DC-bias derating impact on regulator output capacitance budget.
