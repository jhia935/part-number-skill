# TDK CGA Series (Automotive MLCC)

[[tdk]]'s AEC-Q200-qualified automotive MLCC family, the auto-grade analogue of the commercial [[octopart-tdk-cga8l3c0g-product-guide|C-series]]. CGA = Capacitor Ceramic Automotive Grade. The most relevant TDK MLCC line for any application that will see ≥ 125 °C ambient, vibration, or thermal-cycle stress beyond commercial limits.

## Variants
- **CGA general grade** — full case-size + temperature-characteristic matrix (C0G, X7R, X8R) AEC-Q200 qualified.
- **CGA soft-termination** — conductive resin layer between Cu termination and Ni barrier for flex-crack mitigation up to 5 mm PCB flex; AEC-Q200.
- **CGA conductive-epoxy** — AgPdCu termination for conductive-glue mounting, reduced silver migration risk.
- **CGA mid-voltage / high-voltage** — purpose-built for automotive battery line, OBC, BMS applications. Marketed as "Excellent DC bias properties" — engineered to flatten the VCC sigmoid.
